Our story

Westbrook Bakery was born in 2002 out of a love for all things cake. Since opening our doors, we have expanded our menu to include bread, pastry, pie, and cookies, along with a few other daily specialties.

We started the catering arm of our business in 2010. We love learning about our client's events and making sure we provide the best possible treats for any event.

Still family owned and operated, Westbrook Bakery has three locations in the Salt Lake Valley to keep your tastebuds happy.

Come taste for yourself!
