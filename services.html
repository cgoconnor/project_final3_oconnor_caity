Class FAQ

  • Q: How much do classes cost?
    • A: Demonstrations are free, but you must register in advance, as space is limited. Decorating classes are $25.00. This covers the cost of supplies
  • Q: Do I need to bring anything?
    • A: For demonstrations, you do not need to bring anything, except a pen and notebook to take notes if you wish. For decorating classes, the cost of the class includes supplies. However, you are welcome to bring your own.
  • Q: Do I get to sample the treats?
    • A: Yes! For demonstrations, we'll make sure you get a sample of whatever we make. For decorating classes, you get to take home the mini-cake you decorate.
