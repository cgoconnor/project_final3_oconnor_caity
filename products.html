Our tiered and specialty cakes come in chocolate, white, spice, and strawberry flavors. They can be filled with fruit and cream, buttercream, or whipped cream. All tiered cakes are iced with buttercream, but you can add a fondant covering at an extra charge.

We price our specialty cakes on a case-by-case basis, but you can see the table below for our base prices (no fondant or decoration).
